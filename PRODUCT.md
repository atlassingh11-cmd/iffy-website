# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Iffy serves Dubai and Abu Dhabi property buyers, sellers, investors, relocators, and end-users. Most arrive on mobile while comparing agents, areas, developments, or routes to market. They need to decide whether Iffy is credible, candid, locally informed, and worth contacting before they will share details or book a conversation.

## Product Purpose

The site positions Iffy Khan as a trusted property advisor, not a listings portal or a high-pressure salesperson. It should build confidence quickly, explain how he helps buyers and sellers make better decisions, and convert qualified interest into direct enquiries through consultation, WhatsApp, phone, or email. The area guides and advisory tools should support discovery without distracting from the relationship with Iffy.

## Brand Personality

Assured, perceptive, and human. The experience should feel technically sharp and visually progressive while remaining calm enough for high-value decisions. The voice is direct, plain-spoken, and willing to advise against a poor deal.

## Anti-references

- Generic luxury-agent black-and-gold styling with little evidence or personality.
- Repetitive bordered cards, icon grids, eyebrow labels, and other template-like landing-page scaffolding.
- Purple glow effects, gradient text, decorative glass panels, or visual effects with no conversion purpose.
- Invented listings, awards, metrics, testimonials, or market claims.
- A listings marketplace that makes Iffy feel interchangeable with a portal.
- A slow cinematic experience that hides content, blocks navigation, or ignores reduced-motion preferences.

## Design Principles

1. Put the advisor before the inventory. Iffy's judgment, process, and presence are the primary product.
2. Make confidence tangible. Use real people, real locations, clear credentials, and specific process details instead of generic promises.
3. Give buyers and sellers distinct routes without splitting the site into two disconnected experiences.
4. Use motion to reveal meaning. The arc reveal, zoom parallax, and glass treatments should guide attention and never become decoration.
5. Convert without pressure. Every major decision point should offer a clear, low-friction route to a direct conversation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA for contrast, keyboard access, focus visibility, semantic structure, form labeling, and touch targets. All scroll and entrance effects need complete reduced-motion fallbacks, and content must remain available when JavaScript or animation fails.
